Queens Land Amusement Park, Chennai Queens Land Amusement Park is a large theme park and water park established in 2003, located on the Chennai-Bangalore Trunk Road in Poonamallee, near your current location. Address: 22JH+229, Chennai-Bengaluru Highway Palanjur Sembarambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600123. Attractions: The park is spread over 70 acres and features 51 rides, including 33 for adults and 18 for children. Notable rides include the Free Fall Tower (one of the tallest in Southeast Asia), the Alpen Blitz, the Himalayan Water Ride, and the new "Niagara Falls" attraction. The park has both dry and water rides, as well as separate swimming pools for men and women.
